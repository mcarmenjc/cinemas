    Transport information:

    By train
    North Greenwich station on the Jubilee line is the closest tube station from the complex. DLR stations Canary Wharf, Cutty Sark and Greenwich are situated near the Cinema. Emirates Airline cable cars run between North Greenwich and the Royal Dock with a journey time of less than 10 minutes. The cable car provides easy access to The O2 and Excel Centre.

    By bus
    Bus routes 108, 472, 422, 486 serve the complex.

    By car
    The M25 (London orbital), the A2/A20 (from the South East) and the M11 (from the North) all offer good access and The O2 is clearly signposted.

    Parking
    Cineworld ticket holders get 4 hours free parking in Car Parks 2,3 or 4. The 4 hour allocation begins on entry to the car park and free parking will only be valid once your film has finished. To obtain free parking take your unpaid parking ticket and cinema stub to the Parking Office (opposite Car Park 2) for validation. Customers who exceed the 4 hour limit will be required to pay for any additional time in the car park. The parking ticket is valid for one trip per day only. Should any of the above Car Parks be full, Car Park 1 can be used, in those instances only, as the free parking does not apply to Car Park 1.

    There is also the North Greenwich London Underground NCP car park, please check www.ncp.co.uk – North Greenwich Stn for pricing. Please note free parking does not apply to the NCP. Cineworld does not own or operate any car park and conditions of use are subject to change.

    Disabled Access
    Full wheelchair access throughout the cinema and into all screens.

    Disabled Parking
    Disabled bays available in The O2 car parks.
